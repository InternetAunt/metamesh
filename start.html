Getting Started with MetaMesh

Follow these steps to dive into the decentralized world of MetaMesh.

Step 1: Set Up Your Wallet

Download and install a crypto wallet like MetaMask to interact with MetaMesh.

Step 2: Connect to MetaMesh

Connect your wallet to MetaMesh by clicking on the 'Connect Wallet' button on the homepage.

Step 3: Explore and Create

Start exploring, creating, and interacting with decentralized applications on MetaMesh.
